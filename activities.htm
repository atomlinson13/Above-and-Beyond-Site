Things to do in the Penticton Area

  • Skaha Lake Park- Enjoy activities such as camping, climbing, fishing, and dog friendly beaches at Skaha Park. The park also offers a playground and water park aswell as basketball and tennis courts.
  • Wineries- Many BC wineries thrive in the Okanagan and many wine tours are offered locally. Some popular local wineries include Painted Rock Estate Winery, Poplar Grove, and Hillside Winery. If you wish to book a wine tour please let us known and we can set this up for you.
  • Apex Mountain- A Ski Resort located in Penticton offers lift passes, ski and snowboard rentals, lessons, cross country sking, snowshoeing, tubing, and night sking.
  • Skaha Bluffs Provincal Park- Enjoy the beautiful mountains and hiking trails in this large provincal park located in Penticton. The park is dog friendly and also has many biking and horseback riding trails.

Family Fun Activities
