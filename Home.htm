Welcome

L ocated at the mountain top overlooking beautiful Skaha lake, lies this award winning home. Built by green builder Rivendell Homes, it is heated and cooled by geothermal energy. Starting this winter season get great rates on rooms and accomodations. This 7000 square foot home sits on over an acre of property. The back of the home includes a concrete patio, outdoor kitchen, and in ground pool. There are multiple patio areas around the back of the house that all enjoy breathtaking views of Skaha Lake and the vineyards. Above & Beyond is owned by Gary and Christa Tomlinson. Gary is a successful entrepreneuar in the mining industry and runs his own company out of the home. Christa grew up in Germany and speaks fluent German and English. She also plays an important role in Gary's business and managing the B&B. The couple have three small dogs together, Charlie, Bitsy, and Shorty. Guests are welcome to bring their dogs, provided they are friendly and under control. Children are also welcome at Above & Beyond. You can find our room rentals and availabilty on our rates page.

Penticton is home to some very prestigious wineries which you can view and check out in the activities section of the website. Above & Beyond hosts a morning breakfast every day at 9am provided by Christa who has extensive chef experience. Some amenities that are available to all guests at Above & Beyond are the use of the pool, outdoor bar/BBQ area, gym, theatre room, and steam shower. The house also has central air conditioning and heated floors. Free Wi-Fi is provided for all guests and they have access to all the 4 seating areas in the house. Bike rentals are available whether you enjoy mountain biking or street riding. Three kayaks are available for rent for those who wish to explore the lake. There is also a Mercedes luxury motorhome or Mercedes SUVs available for rent. Please visit our contact page and message us for any inquiries about bike, kayak, car, or motorhome rentals.
